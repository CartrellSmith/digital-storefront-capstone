Our Story

Founded in Olive Branch, Mississippi, Kannon’s Home Furnitures & Decor has been crafting beautiful, durable furniture since 1987. What started as a small family workshop has grown into a trusted destination for homeowners seeking quality furniture and stylish décor. At Kannon’s, we believe a home should be comfortable, welcoming, and full of personality. Every piece in our collection reflects our commitment to craftsmanship, attention to detail, and timeless design—from cozy living rooms to elegant dining spaces. Now run by the second generation of the Kannon family, we continue to combine classic techniques with modern trends, helping families turn their houses into homes they love.
